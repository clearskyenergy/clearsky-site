Membership

Two ways to join the coalition

Whether you build storage projects or you review them for a municipality, membership puts you on the same page — literally. Pick the track that fits your role and register in a couple of minutes.

Developers & EPCs

Submit a permit-ready package and track it all the way to approval — no chasing email threads, no lost revisions.

  • Submit permits with plans, one-lines, and documents in one place.
  • Track status and respond to review comments as they come in.
  • Push from the Site Map Designer straight into a submission.

Municipalities & zoning boards

Give your jurisdiction a single, organized queue for storage permits — with everything the reviewer needs attached to each one.

  • Review submissions in a consistent, complete format every time.
  • Comment & request changes without leaving the portal.
  • Approve with a clear record of who decided what, and when.

Questions

Coalition FAQ

Who can become a member?

Energy storage developers and EPCs, plus municipal permitting offices and zoning boards. If you submit or review storage permits, the portal is for you.

Does it cost anything?

Participating AHJs join free. Reach out and we'll walk you through developer access for your organization.

How do developers and AHJs stay in sync?

Both work from the same submission record, so comments, revisions, and approvals are visible to the right people in real time.

Can I bring my existing site plans?

Yes. Upload your documents, or hand a layout off directly from the ClearSky Site Map Designer.

How long does onboarding take?

Developers can register and submit the same day. Jurisdictions are set up with reviewer accounts and a short walkthrough.

Is my data restricted to my role?

Yes. Role-based access means developers and zoning boards each see only what's relevant to them.
