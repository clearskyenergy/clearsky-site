What OMEGA does

One tool for the entire development stack

Most developers stitch together CAD, GIS, spreadsheets, and a permitting consultant. OMEGA collapses that into a single browser-based platform where every stage feeds the next — the site map drives the one-line, the one-line drives the permit set, and the equipment selection drives the economics.

01 · SITE INTELLIGENCE

Site Map Designer

Drop a coordinate or address and OMEGA pulls a frozen satellite basemap, then lets you draw parcel boundaries, BESS pads, setbacks, and equipment with real-world scale locked in. Multi-pad placement, automatic setback checks, and a live summary that counts every pad you draw.

  • Frozen satellite capture with true scale
  • Parcel + APN boundary and setback layers
  • Multi-pad BESS layout with equipment assemblies
Site Map Designer
02 · ELECTRICAL

One-Line Diagram Builder

OMEGA generates a code-aligned electrical one-line straight from your equipment selection — BESS containers, PCS / bidirectional inverters, interconnection point, and revenue meter — matched to the topology of the site you drew. Integrated PCS logic kicks in automatically for GRID-series systems above 760 kW.

  • Auto-built from selected equipment
  • PCS / inverter and meter placement
  • Exports as a standalone diagram sheet

03 · PERMITTING

Permit & Interconnection Packages

OMEGA assembles a full ANSI D engineering set — cover, electrical (E0–E3), and structural (S1.1–S3.0) sheets — plus interconnection packages framed to Illinois Admin Code Title 83, Part 466 and ComEd's BESH / SDVPP pathways. Signage schedules, interconnection standards compliance, and revenue-meter details are generated inline.

  • 10-slide ANSI D permit set per project
  • ComEd BESH submittal package generation
  • Interconnection framework compliance built in

05 · THE OMEGA ENGINE

AI at the core

OMEGA pairs two AI engines through a secure serverless layer. A reasoning model parses utility bills and interconnection requirements and drafts permit notes; a generative model renders AI-enhanced technical stencils and blueprint drawings. The result is a Guided Build Wizard that walks a project from configuration to export.

  • AI utility-bill parsing and permit-note drafting
  • Generative stencil + blueprint rendering

Why OMEGA is different

Built for the way energy projects actually move

Runs anywhere, instantly

A single self-contained web application — no installs, no plugins. Works on a laptop in the office or an iPad at the site walk, with offline-capable PWA support.

One source of truth

Site geometry, equipment, economics, and permits all reference the same project model. Change the battery and the one-line, the cost, and the proforma update together.

AI-native, not bolted on

Reasoning and generative models are wired into the workflow through a secure serverless proxy — parsing documents, drafting notes, and rendering drawings as part of the build.

Regulator-ready output

Permit sets and interconnection packages are framed to real AHJ and utility standards — the same outputs that backed ClearSky's submitted ComEd BESS site packages.

Economics you can defend

Real catalog pricing, current incentive logic, and stacked-revenue modeling mean the proforma reflects a project you could actually finance — not a rough estimate.
