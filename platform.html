What OMEGA does

One tool for the entire development stack

Most developers stitch together CAD, GIS, spreadsheets, and a permitting consultant. OMEGA collapses that into a single browser-based platform where every stage feeds the next — the site map drives the one-line, the one-line drives the permit set, and the equipment selection drives the economics.

01 · SITE INTELLIGENCE

Site Map Designer

Drop a coordinate or address and OMEGA pulls a frozen satellite basemap, then lets you draw parcel boundaries, BESS pads, setbacks, and equipment with real-world scale locked in. Multi-pad placement, automatic setback checks, and a live summary that counts every pad you draw.

Project flow

From customer intake to optimized asset

OMEGA runs the whole path — a customer or partner submits a site, AI evaluates and packages it, the platform optimizes the design and revenue stack, and the project moves into managed operations. Every stage is instrumented, so nothing gets re-keyed and nothing falls through.

STEP 01

Customer & Project Intake

A client or partner submits a site through a guided intake — location, load, program interest, and offtake. It lands as a structured project, not an email thread.

STEP 02

AI Evaluation & Packaging

OMEGA's AI screens viability, parses utility data, drafts the one-line and permit set, and builds the interconnection package — turning a raw site into a submittable project.

STEP 03

Optimization

The platform sizes equipment, optimizes the revenue stack across SDVPP, regulation, arbitrage, and offtake, and finds the configuration with the strongest financeable return.

STEP 04

Delivery & Managed Ops

Approved projects move into construction and operating asset management — with monitoring, dispatch, and performance data flowing back into the same platform.
