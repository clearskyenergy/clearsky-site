Where ClearSky is headed

Microgrids & micro data centers powered by energy arbitrage

ClearSky's core thesis: co-locate battery storage with distributed compute to build self-contained microgrids that buy energy when it's cheap, serve compute continuously, and sell capacity and regulation back to the grid when it's valuable. Energy arbitrage funds the compute; the compute anchors the load; the grid services stack on top. It's a battery-first model that doesn't depend on solar and turns every site into a dual-purpose energy node and edge data center.
