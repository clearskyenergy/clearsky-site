The concept

One meter. Two assets working for you.

🔋

Asset One · Energy

A smart battery

A 13 kWh battery charges overnight when power is cheap and discharges during grid peaks. Your utility pays for that flexibility through Virtual Power Plant programs — and you keep a share of the savings and payments.

Earns fromVPP dispatch + arbitrage
🧠

Asset Two · Compute

An edge AI node

A small GPU node attaches to your battery and reads the excess power it isn't using. It sells that spare capacity as AI compute to a marketplace of companies that need GPU power — turning idle energy into cash.

Earns fromAI compute marketplace
